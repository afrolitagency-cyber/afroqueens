The Sound That Conquered the World

A decade ago, Afrobeats was a regional phenomenon known primarily to diaspora communities and a handful of adventurous music journalists. Today, it is inescapable — on Spotify editorial playlists, in Hollywood film scores, at global fashion weeks, and on stadium stages from London to Los Angeles.

The question that fascinates us is not whether Afrobeats conquered the world, but how — and more importantly, on whose terms.
